Choose Your Adventure

Addendum

- Project created as a Choose Your Own Adventure game using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
- The planning phase involved designing an intricate story flow with multiple paths and endings.
- The HTML and CSS provided a structured and visually appealing layout, while JavaScript's flexibility facilitated dynamic story progression.
- Challenges were met through careful organization of the story object and thoughtful event handling.
- The project highlighted the importance of clear storytelling in interactive experiences.
- Deepened understanding of web development tools and techniques through practical application.
- Each point was approached with a focus on user experience and engagement.
- Insights gained include the power of JavaScript for interactive narratives and the importance of cohesive design across HTML, CSS, and JS.
